About
Art Direction and design, for me, is about never ceasing to look for the energy, constantly redoing, refining and testing. When comparing alternatives I try to put into words which options serve which purpose. If the purpose is clear, then the decision makes itself. That’s how I move forward. For me, the ultimate thrill lies in discovering the right decisions and gradually revealing an idea’s full, glimmering potential.

At the moment, I’m working on my final projects at Beckmans College of Design, leading up to my exam in June 2025.
